Exhibition Avenue in Manama, Bahrain is a place of a thousand shops. No doubt that one of them has what you are looking for at a great price. Good luck finding that shop!
There are a thousand Exhibition Avenues across the world. Barebone. Useful. Hoora's goal is to provide a simple framework for the microentreprenuers of the Exhibition Avenues of the world to make it easy for customers to find that one thing.
